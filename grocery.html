Understanding the chart

  1. Actual Sales: The data input or the historical data of sales of the company
  2. Predicted Sales: The next 365 day sale prediction with respect to the historical data
  3. Trend: The general sale trend of the company
  4. Seasonality: The Yearly trend of the given company
